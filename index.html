Who he is

From The Chair
To The Stage

Gum Cruz is widely regarded as one of the top barbers in the Philippines — a global barbering educator, TEDx speaker, creative collaborator, and industry leader elevating Filipino craftsmanship, culture, and style. He is the founder of Pomade Cartel Barbershop and Mizutani Scissors Philippines.

Whether he’s cutting at Pomade Cartel, training a barbershop fleet, or speaking onstage at an expo, Gum brings the same attention to detail across every environment. Pomade Cartel places strong emphasis on technical barbering fundamentals and personalized service — and every barber inside the shop is trained using the same standards Gum applies to his own work.

The journey

Career Highlights

  1. 2021

    Uncomplacent Barbering Seminars launched

    Started in Angeles City, expanding education and training across cities in the years that followed. Also a finalist in the Mizutani World Cutting Photo Contest (Japan) and competitor in the OP Barberworld Annual Competition (UK).

  2. 2022

    Mizutani Japan exclusive partner

    Became the exclusive Philippine partner of Mizutani Japan, and earned recognition as a finalist for Behind The Chair (USA).

  3. 2023

    Philippine Barber Convention

    Co-organized the Philippine Barber Convention in Pampanga — one of the biggest community infrastructure milestones for the local barbering scene.

  4. 2024

    First Filipino WAHL SEA Educator

    Became the first Filipino WAHL Southeast Asia educator, representing the Philippines in WAHL trainings and expo demos across Malaysia, Thailand, and beyond.

  5. 2025

    TEDx Talk: ‘Why I’m Obsessed With Barbering’

    Took the stage to share how discipline, humility, and consistency from the barber’s chair translate into a broader philosophy of craft and growth.
